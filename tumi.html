About Tumi

Founded in 1975, Tumi has established itself as the premier luxury travel and lifestyle brand, known for its innovative design, superior craftsmanship, and exceptional functionality. Tumi's commitment to creating products that enhance the travel experience has made it the choice of discerning business travelers and luxury consumers worldwide.

From their signature ballistic nylon to their cutting-edge polycarbonate designs, Tumi combines timeless elegance with modern innovation. Each piece is crafted with precision and attention to detail, ensuring that form and function work in perfect harmony to meet the demands of today's global travelers.
