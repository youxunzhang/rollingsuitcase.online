About Briggs & Riley

Founded in 1993, Briggs & Riley has established itself as a leading name in the luggage industry, known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. With decades of experience in creating travel solutions, Briggs & Riley continues to push the boundaries of design and functionality.

From their earliest designs to their latest innovations, Briggs & Riley has maintained a focus on creating luggage that meets the diverse needs of modern travelers. Their commitment to quality craftsmanship and innovative design has made them a trusted choice for travelers worldwide.
