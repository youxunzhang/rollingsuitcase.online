About Samsonite

Founded in 1910, Samsonite has been at the forefront of luggage innovation for over a century. As the world's leading luggage manufacturer, Samsonite combines cutting-edge technology with timeless design to create travel solutions that meet the needs of modern travelers.

From lightweight polycarbonate shells to advanced security features, Samsonite's commitment to quality and innovation has made it the trusted choice for millions of travelers worldwide. Their extensive product range includes everything from carry-on luggage to large checked bags, all designed with durability and functionality in mind.
