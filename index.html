Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find the right repair center for my suitcase brand? +

Simply click on your suitcase brand in our brands section to access authorized repair center contact information, including phone numbers, addresses, and email contacts for your region.

Are the repair services covered under warranty? +

Warranty coverage depends on your suitcase's original warranty terms and the type of repair needed. Our service centers will assess your case and inform you about warranty eligibility before starting any work.

How long does a typical suitcase repair take? +

Most common repairs like wheel replacement, handle fixes, and zipper repairs are completed within 24-48 hours. More complex repairs may take 3-5 business days, depending on parts availability.

Do you offer international repair services? +

Yes! Our network includes authorized service centers across North America, Europe, and major international destinations. Premium and Travel Pro members have access to priority international services.

Can I track my repair status online? +

Premium Plus and Travel Pro members can track repair status through our mobile app and online portal. You'll receive real-time updates on your repair progress and estimated completion times.
